Privacy Policy

We here at Golden Pest Control respect your privacy and will not share any of your personal data. This Privacy Policy describes what data we receive, use, and how we protect your privacy.

1. Data We Receive

Name, Contact Information (Telephone, Email, and Address): We collect your name, telephone number, email address, and delivery address if you complete the forms on our website or when you call us for pest control. We may also ask for payment information to bill.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to process your requests, deliver pest control, contact you, and send you offers (when you give us your consent). We do not disclose your information to third parties outside of service providers who help us provide our services.

3. Data Protection

We take appropriate technical and organizational security measures to guard your information against unauthorized access, destruction, or alteration. However, no method of Internet data transfer is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Updating Your Personal Information

You can check, change, or delete your personal information at any time. You may exercise these rights by contacting us at the contact information below.

5. Disclosure of Personal Data

We might share your personal data with trusted third-party service providers who help us run our website and provide pest control services. These third parties have a duty of confidentiality towards you.

6. Cookies Policy

Cookies are used on our site to enhance your experience. Cookies store your preferences and help speed up the website. You can block cookies in your browser options, but this might impact your visit to our website.

7. Periodic Updates to the Privacy Policy

We may amend our privacy policy from time to time. The latest update will be published on this page, and the date of the last update will be displayed at the top of the page.

If you have any questions or concerns about your privacy, you can email us at info@goldenpestcontrol.com.
