Golden Pest Control’s Cookie Policy and GDPR Compliance

Golden Pest Control stands with you and supports the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) to protect your data.

1. Data Collection and Processing

We do not collect any personal information unless necessary for us to deliver our services. This includes your email address and any other information you voluntarily submit via our site. Your data is collected if you consent or in the service of our agreement with you.

2. Your GDPR Data Rights

Following are your rights in terms of GDPR with respect to your data:

  • Right to access: You can ask us for a copy of the information about you that we hold.
  • Freedom to rectify: You can request that we rectify inaccurate data of yours.
  • Right to erasure: You can request that we erase your data when it is no longer required or when you withdraw consent.
  • Right to data portability: You can request for your data to be sent to a different service provider as machine-readable data.
  • Right to objection: You may also object to direct marketing based on your data.

If you wish to exercise any of these rights, you can write to us at +1 2018912688.

3. Cookie Use

We use cookies on our website for technical purposes, to monitor website traffic, and to customize your experience. A cookie is a small file stored on your computer. You can adjust cookie options in your browser settings. However, certain parts of our site may not function properly if you block cookies.

If you use our website, you authorize us to use cookies as described in this policy.

4. Retention of Personal Data

We will retain your personal data as long as we are required to do so in order to provide our services or as permitted by law. When we no longer require your data, we delete or anonymize it.

5. Please Contact Us

In case you have any queries or complaints regarding GDPR and our cookie policy, please email us at info@goldenpestcontrol.com.
